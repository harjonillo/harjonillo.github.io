Adapted from [Michael's chocolate chip cookies](https://thegreatbritishbakeoff.co.uk/recipes/all/michael-chocolate-chip-cookies/)
(GBBO) — with browned butter, Malagos dark chocolate, and an air fryer.

1. Brown a third of the butter. Cool it over a bowl of ice while beating; the
   rest of the butter should be soft, not melted. Combine.
2. Cream the butter with 150 g of the brown sugar (and the salt, if using
   unsalted) until light and fluffy.
3. Beat in the egg, yolk, and vanilla until fully combined.
4. Sift the flour, cornflour, and baking soda together, then fold in — don't
   overwork the dough.
5. Fold in the chocolate and macadamias.
6. Cover and chill for 48 hours. Non-negotiable.
7. Soften to room temperature, then fold in the remaining sugar until evenly
   distributed.
8. Bake about 6 cookies at a time (1 tbsp of dough each): air fryer, 190 °C,
   7 minutes.
9. Cool until they firm up a little. Serve warm, at room temperature, or
   freeze for later.
